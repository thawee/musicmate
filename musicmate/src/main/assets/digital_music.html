Music Encodings

High Quality Audio
Best compromise between data usage and sound fidelity.
Format: MP3, AAC
Resolution: 16bit/48kHz, bitrate 256kbps or higher
Lossless Audio
Reproduce details of music smooth as CD quality that you can hear.
Format: FLAC, ALAC, WAVE, AIFF
Resolution: 16bit/48kHz, 24bit/48kHz
Recommended Genre: Pop, Animation, Rock
Hi-Res Lossless Audio
Enjoy and Listening the rich music which reproduces fine details of musical instruments. Distinguish each string vibration of the stringed instrument.
Format: FLAC, ALAC, WAVE, AIFF
Resolution: 24bit/88kHz, 24bit/96kHz or Higher
Recommended Genre: Classic, Jazz, Vocal, Soundtrack, Ballad
Direct Stream Digital (DSD)
You can hear the detail and wide range of the music, its warm tone is very enjoyable.
Format: DSF, DFF
Resolution: DSD 64, DSD 128, DSD 256 or Higher
Recommended Genre: Classic, Jazz, Vocals
MQA Authentication (MQA)
MQA is distributing technology for high-quality recordings, to bring listeners the sound of the studio and older recordings from the Archive.
  - MQA (green light) indicate the stream is genuine, provenance may be uncertain or that it is not yet the final release.
  - MQA Studio (blue light) gives confirmation directly from mastering engineers, producers or artists to their listeners.

DYNAMIC RANGE

Loudness Meter

Integrated
Shows the average loudness measured from start to stop. The recommended value for the integrated loudness is -23 LUFS.
Range
Shows the dynamic range of the audio measured from start to stop. This value indicate how much dynamic compression you can hear, varied for each music genre.
  - Classical music typically offers between 20 dB and 32 dB
  - jazz generally varied from 13 dB to 23 dB
  - Pop, Rock, R&B, Hip-Hop and Country music typically around 6 dB to 10 dB
True Peak
Shows the true peak level of the audio. The maximum permitted true peak level in production is -1 dB.
